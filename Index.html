Glossary of Grocery Store Items

Bread
Used for sandwhiches, and can used as toast.
Butter
An ingredient used in many cooking recipes, but not usually consumed by itself.
Candy
A wide variety of sweets and chocolates. Contains a high amount of sugar.
Cereal
A box containing hundreds of it's type of cereal. Common breakfast item.
Cheese
Comes in different types such as cheddar and swiss. Is also used in cooking and grilling.
Chips
Junk food item that comes in many different bags. Contains tens of the same type of brand of chip.
Crackers
Similar to chips, but usually contains more carbohydrates.
Drinks
There's a massive variety of drinks. Examples include juice, soda, coffee, and even water.
Eggs
Healthy source of protein while also being used as an ingredient.
Flour
Never consumed by itself, but it's a main ingredient in baking.
Fruits
Examples of fruits include apples, bananas, peaches, pears, and pineapple. One of the healthiest ingredients in a grocery store.
Ice Cream
A cold desert packed with sugar. The least healthy item on the list.
Meats
Major source of protein. Meats can come in deli form like ham, or raw meats to be cooked for burgers and steaks.
Milk
The healthiest drink at a grocery store besides water. It's also a common ingredient in cooking and baking.
Sauces
A source of flavor for a wide variety of foods. Sauces also come in many different forms including condiments like ketchup and mustard.
Vegetables
The final item in both the healthy list and glossary. It's the most healthy item you can buy in a grocery store. Examples include carrots, broccoli, Potatoes, and Cucumbers.
